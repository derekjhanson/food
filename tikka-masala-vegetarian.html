Tikka Masala (vegetarian)

The Hansons

The veggie option

AmountIngredient
2 tbspbutter
1/2medium onion
3cloves of garlic
1 tbspginger
2 tspgaram masala
1 tspcurry
1/2 tspchipotle chilis
1/4 tspcumin
1/2 tsphimalayan pink salt
28 oz candiced tomatoes
2 tbspmaple syrup
1cauliflower head
8 ozyogurt (honey - noosa)
  • Saute butter, garlic, ginger, onion for 3-4 minutes.
  • Add ~ 2 tbsp water and the spices, stir while cooking for another minute or so
  • Add diced tomatoes, cauliflower, maple syrup (yogurt?) and pressure cook for 2 minutes
  • Serve with rice (or naan) - make sure you're cooking this at the same time! :)
