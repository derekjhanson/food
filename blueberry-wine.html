Blueberry Wine

by The Hansons
published on January 5, 2020

Homemade blueberry wine (https://practicalselfreliance.com/blueberry-wine/)

AmountIngredient
3 lbsblueberries (one large frozen bag)
2 1/4 lbs (4 1/2 cups)sugar
1/4 cuplemon juice (acid)
1/8 cupraisins (yeast nutrient)
1 cupblack tea (tannin)
1/4 packagechampagne yeast
2 tbsoak chips (optional)

Â Allow blueberries to reach near room temperature from frozen (frozen berries release more juice after cell walls burst).

Add sugar, then muddle the berries into the sugar (mash)

After 6-12 hours, pour one quart of boiling water over the mixture, stir

Cool to room temperature

Primary: Stir entire mixture once a day for 5-7 days

Secondary: Siphon then ferment 4-6 weeks

Tertiary: Either add Campden tablet and ferment until clear (another few weeks) or give it 6-8 months to naturally clear

Bottle: bottle and let age for several more months
