Savory Smushed Potatoes

by The Hansons
published on September 18, 2019

Tired of regular baked or mashed potatoes? Try this hybrid packed with flavor.

IngredientAmount
Salt2 tsp
Pepper1 tsp
Thyme2 tsp
Paprika1 1/2 tsp
Oregano1 tsp
Onion Powder1 tsp
Garlic1/2 tsp

Mix up the spices, then brush olive oil onto potatoes and dust with the seasoning.
