Risotto w/Leaves

by The Hansons
published on September 30, 2019

Almost creamy risotto with leaves and beans: a unique take on the rice and beans staple.

Saute onions in olive oil. Then add rice, stirring to get oil coating on it (like popcorn). Then add water and pressure cook for 4 minutes.

While cooking, heat beans and leaves in separate pot.

To finish, add dash of salt to rice, mix in beans and leaves. On your plate, sprinkle with cheese on top for a gooey delicious take on rice and beans. Pairs well with chicken bratwurst.
