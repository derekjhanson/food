Pulled Pork Mac N' Cheese

by The Hansons
published on September 30, 2019

 

Mix up spices and set aside. Sear all sides of roast in pan with ~ 2tbs olive oil. Rub in spice mix onto the roast.

Fill instant pot with 1 1/2 cups water and 1 1/2 cups and the trivet. Place roast on top of the trivet and cook 60 minutes (4 lbs).

Stubbs' Sticky Sweet BBQ sauce

# The Rub

AmountIngredient

> 1 tsppaprika
1/2 tsp garlic powder

2 tspsalt
1 tsppepper
1/3 tspcayenne

 # The Rest
