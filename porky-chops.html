Porky Chops

by The Hansons
published on October 23, 2019
AmountIngredient
1lbpork chops
2 tbsbrown sugar
1 tspsalt
1 tsppepper
1 tsppaprika
1/2 tsponion powder
2 tbsbutter
1 1/4 cupwater
1 tspmaple syrup

 

Add tbs butter to pot, turn on saute, wait until hot before adding seasoned meat

Saute 1-2 minutes per side after seasoning

Add 1 1/4 cup water and ~1 tsp maple syrup

Pressure cook 7 minutes (1lb of pork chops)

 

Options: liquid smoke, worcestershire sauce
