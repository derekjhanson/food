Jimdak (Korean Braised Chicken)

by The Hansons
published on September 17, 2019

Â 

A savory Korean treat, but putting this together takes some prep time and the outcome depends heavily upon the order in which the tasks are completed.

  • Cut chicken into bite-sized chunks, then place into a pot covered in water

  • Boil the water and chicken for 5 minutes

  • Drain the water

  • Add sauce, boil for 10 minutes

  • Add potatoes, carrots, and onions, boil for another 7 minutes

  • Reduce heat, simmer for 8 minutes

  • Add the glass noodles (or green bean noodles) and green onions. Ensure not too much liquid before putting in noodles or they will be soggy

  • Serve ~ 1 minute after adding noodles

The Sauce:

IngredientAmount
Water1 1/2 cup
Coconut Aminos1/3 cup
Brown Sugar2 tbs
Honey1 tbs
Ginger1/2 tsp (minced)
Sesame Oil2 tsp
