Purple Potatoes

by The Hansons
published on September 22, 2019

Chop potatoes into evenish chunks. Removal of skin is optional.

In instant pot, add 1 1/4 cup water, 2 large purple potatoes, dash of chives, dash of garlic powder, 1 tbsp butter - pressure cook 10 minutes

Add cream cheese or milk to soften potatoes (make fluffier) - amount?

 

Made: 9/22/19 with steak and buttered asparagus
