Chicken Tortilla Soup

by The Hansons
published on November 8, 2019

A crock pot meal with hidden heat.

AmountIngredient
3 tbsButter
1/2Onion
2 clovesGarlic
2 cupsBroth/Water
1 lbChicken Breasts
2 tspCumin
1 tspChili Powder
1 tspOregano
1 tspTaco Seasoning
14 ozRed Enchilada Sauce
1-2 cupsHeavy Cream
SprigsCilantro
CanCorn (optional)

Â Add butter to saute pan, cook with onion, garlic.

Add base ingredients to crock pot, then add chicken breasts on top. Cook on high for 3-4 hours.

When complete, shred the chicken with a fork. Then add the heavy cream? Cook on high for another hour. Serve with cilantro garnish and tortilla strips.
