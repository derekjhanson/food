Corn on the Cob

by The Hansons
published on September 18, 2019

In the instant pot, simply toss in the trivet, a hint over 1 cup of water, then seal and set to pressure cook for 2 minutes.

Garnish options:

Mexican Street Corn - Dry

  • lime, cheese, chili powder or tajin seasoning

Mexican Street Corn - Wet

  • lime, cheese, chili powder, mayo and or sour cream

Taiwanese Street Corn - Wet

- 3 tablespoons light soy sauce (not low sodium soy sauce)
2 tablespoons Shacha sauce (Chinese: 沙茶醬)
1 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon garlic powder
¼ teaspoon sesame oil

 

  • For the above, can either brush onto corn and bake 5-10 minutes @ 425 
  • OR simply cut off corn from cob, place into bowl and mix in the ingredients like a salad
