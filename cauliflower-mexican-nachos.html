Cauliflower Mexican "Nachos"

by The Hansons
published on January 8, 2020

A nacho delight using seasoned cauliflower instead of chips.

AmountIngredient
3 tbsgrapeseed oil
3/4 tspchili powder
3/4 tspcumin
3/4 tsponion powder
1/4 tspsalt
8 cupscauliflower (chopped)
several sprigscilantro
1/8chopped red onion
1 canblack beans
1 1/2 cupsshredded chicken (pre-cooked)
desired levelMexican cheese

 Preheat oven to 400 degrees.

Combine oil, chili powder, cumin, onion powder, salt in large bowl. Toss in cauliflower to coat. 

Spread cauliflower on pre-sprayed baking sheet and cook for 15-20 minutes (15 in gas oven).

When cauliflower starting to brown, pull out of oven, add black beans, chicken (if cold), red onion, and cheese. Bake until cheese melted (~5 min.).

Top with cilantro and / or salsa.
