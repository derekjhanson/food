Carnitas - Dutch Oven

The Hansons

 

  • remove orange halves, onion, and bay leaves
  • simmer to reduce (stirring frequently) until thick and syrupy (around 1 cup liquid) - (8-12 minutes)
  • pull apart meat with forks, season with salt and pepper
  • spread just meat across top of broiler pan, drizzle with remaining syrup
    • broil until meat browned and crispy (only one or two minutes)
    • flip/stir meat, go for another minute or so (can hear sizzle)
