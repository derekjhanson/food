Butternut Squash Chili

by The Hansons
published on November 8, 2019

For those cold nights.

AmountIngredient
LotsButternut Squash
1/4White Onion
dashGarlic Powder
15 ozTomato Sauce
15 ozKidney Beans
15 oz Great Northern Beans
3/4 cupWater
1 lbGround Turkey
1 tspChili Powder
1 tspCumin
1 tspPaprika
1 tspCocoa Powder
3/4 tspSalt
1/4 tspBlack Pepper
SplashMaple Syrup
Big SplashPeach Pecan Whiskey (Old Camp)

 Saute ground turkey and onions 3-5 minutes. If low fat turkey, may need to add splash of water.

Then add all other ingredients and cook for 18 minutes on pressure cook.
