Brandy's Drop Biscuits

by The Hansons
published on January 5, 2020

Yum yum.

AmountIngredient
2 cupsBaking flour
1 tbsbaking powder
1/2 tbssugar
1/4 tspsalt
1/2 cupmelted butter (1 stick)
1 cupmilk
  • heat oven to 450 degrees
  • mix together flur, baking powder, sugar, salt
  • add melted butter and milk, stir just until moistened
  • drop biscuits onto lightly greased baking sheet
  • cook for 10 to 12 minutes or until edges turn golden brown
