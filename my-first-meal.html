Rotisserie Chicken

by The Hansons
published on September 17, 2019

 

Tastes like rotisserie chicken, but done in minutes and with any style (white, dark, mixed) of meat.

  • Rub the rub onto the chicken

  • Saute for ~ 2 minutes in instapot

  • Add > 1 cup water and rest of seasoning to pot

  • Pressure cook for ~ 8 minutes per pound (10 minutes for 1.5 pounds or gets dry)

 

IngredientAmount
Salt2 tsp
Pepper1 tsp
Thyme2 tsp
Paprika1 1/2 tsp
Oregano1 tsp
Onion Powder1 tsp
Garlic Powder1/2 tsp
