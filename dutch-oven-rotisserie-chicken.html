Dutch Oven Rotisserie Chicken

The Hansons

A fall-off-the-bone delicious rotisserie chicken made with relatively little effort.

  • Add olive oil to bottom of dutch oven (less than full coating of bottom).
  • Rinse chicken, remove pieces from cavity, pat dry with paper towel.
  • Chop carrots (~4), onion (1/2).
  • Place chicken in dutch oven breast side down. Sprinkle heavily with Old Bay. Flip chicken breast side up, sprinkle again heavily with Old Bay.
  • Add carrots and onion to sides and inside chicken.
  • Add three to four slices of butter on top of/around chicken.
  • Cook at 375 for just under an hour and a half. Then take off lid and cook for another 5-10 minutes to brown the outside of the chicken.
  • Remove from heat and let rest for at least 10 minutes before cutting (to preserve the juices and prevent meat from drying out).
  • Shred the chicken and save the broth for chicken soup later! (Will need to add water to broth).
